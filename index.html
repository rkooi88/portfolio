About Me

Hi! My name is Rachel Ooi, a sophomore at the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill, studying Computer Science B. S. and Mathematics B. A. with a keen interest in Software Development and Data Analytics

As someone who is passionate about continuous technological innovations, especially about equitable access as a legally blind person, I have demonstrated interest in the tech field as a recent member of Carolina Analytics & Data Science and the Google Developers' Group Chapel Hill. I hope to expand my understanding in various programming languages. I also hope to learn from others about effectively delivering and developing cutting-edge and creative IT projects using Agile methodologies that benefits the world.

Outside of academics, I enjoy being a foodie by seeking out new and delicious restaurants. I also enjoy taking photos of the food, ranging from the most humble--a late-night stop at Panda Express for artificially-colored orange chicken--to the most extraordinary and brag-worthy, like balut, the developed duck embryo still inside its egg that my family tried at a busy Hawker Center in Singapore.
